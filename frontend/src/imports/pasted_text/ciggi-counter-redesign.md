Redesign the "Ciggi Counter" web app with a strong focus on **user behavior and primary action prioritization**.

---

## 🧠 CORE UX PRINCIPLE (VERY IMPORTANT)

This app is used daily, and users primarily:
👉 Open app → Log cigarette count → Close app

Analytics (graphs, trends) are **secondary and occasional use**

Therefore:

* Logging cigarettes must be the **main focus**
* Analytics must be moved to a **separate page**
* Dashboard should feel **fast, frictionless, and action-first**

---

## 🧩 NEW INFORMATION ARCHITECTURE

### 🏠 Page 1: MAIN DASHBOARD (Daily Use)

ONLY include:

* Cig logging (PRIMARY FOCUS)
* Today’s count
* This month count
* Reduction streak

---

### 📊 Page 2: ANALYTICS (Secondary Page)

* Monthly trend graph
* History list
* Advanced stats

Accessible via:
👉 Navbar button: “Analytics” or “Insights”

---

## 🎨 DESIGN STYLE

* Liquid Glass / Frosted Glass UI
* Minimalistic
* Calm and distraction-free
* High focus on usability

---

## 🏠 PAGE 1: MAIN DASHBOARD (CRITICAL DESIGN)

---

### 🔥 HERO SECTION (MOST IMPORTANT)

This is the **centerpiece of the app**

Design a LARGE, visually dominant card:

#### Title:

👉 "Log Today's Cigarettes"

#### Layout:

* Big centered number input
* Large +/- buttons
* Quick add chips: +1, +5, +10
* Primary CTA button: “+ Add Cig”

#### Design Requirements:

* Takes ~40–50% of screen height
* Glassmorphism card
* Strong visual hierarchy
* Slight glow or highlight

#### Interaction:

* Smooth increment/decrement
* Button hover animations
* Feels satisfying to click

---

### 📊 QUICK STATS (SECONDARY)

Place below logging section:

3 cards:

1. 🚬 Today’s Count
2. 📅 This Month
3. 🔥 Reduction Streak

#### Design:

* Smaller than logging section
* Horizontal layout
* Minimal info only (no clutter)

---
